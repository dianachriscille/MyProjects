# EduCore ERP — User Personas

## Persona 1: Maria Santos — School Administrator (Admin)

- **Role**: Admin
- **Age**: 45
- **Background**: School principal with 15+ years in K-12 education in the Philippines. Manages overall school operations.
- **Technical Skill**: Basic — comfortable with email, spreadsheets, and web apps. Not technical.
- **Goals**:
  - Have a single system to manage enrollment, students, and finances
  - See real-time reports on enrollment numbers and outstanding balances
  - Ensure compliance with Philippine Data Privacy Act
  - Control who has access to what in the system
- **Frustrations**:
  - Currently uses spreadsheets and paper forms — data is scattered
  - Cannot easily generate reports for DepEd submissions
  - No audit trail when records are changed
- **Key Workflows**: User management, system configuration, viewing audit logs, generating reports
- **Device**: Desktop computer (Windows), occasionally mobile phone browser

## Persona 2: Jose Reyes — Registrar

- **Role**: Registrar
- **Age**: 32
- **Background**: School registrar handling enrollment, student records, and academic documentation for a K-12 school with 300-500 students.
- **Technical Skill**: Intermediate — uses school management spreadsheets daily, familiar with web applications.
- **Goals**:
  - Process enrollment applications quickly during enrollment season
  - Maintain accurate student records with LRN tracking
  - Easily search and filter students by grade level, section, or status
  - Export student lists for DepEd reporting
- **Frustrations**:
  - Enrollment season is chaotic with paper forms and manual data entry
  - Duplicate student records are hard to detect
  - Cannot track enrollment status (who's approved, who's pending)
- **Key Workflows**: Enter enrollment applications, approve/reject enrollments, manage student profiles, export student lists
- **Device**: Desktop computer (Windows), shared office workstation

## Persona 3: Ana Cruz — Finance Staff

- **Role**: Finance
- **Age**: 38
- **Background**: School finance officer handling tuition billing, payment collection, and financial reporting. Manages accounts for 300-500 students.
- **Technical Skill**: Intermediate — proficient with spreadsheets and basic accounting software.
- **Goals**:
  - Create tuition billing records for enrolled students efficiently
  - Track payments and outstanding balances per student
  - Generate billing summaries for school management
  - Maintain accurate financial records for BIR compliance (future)
- **Frustrations**:
  - Manual billing creation in spreadsheets is error-prone
  - Tracking partial payments across hundreds of students is tedious
  - No easy way to see total outstanding balance across all students
  - Reconciling bank transfer payments with student accounts is slow
- **Key Workflows**: Create individual/bulk billing, record bank transfer payments, view student balances, export billing reports
- **Device**: Desktop computer (Windows), dedicated finance office workstation
